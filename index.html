Projects

This section contains a few of my personal projects, as well as some of my hobbies.

3D Printed Custom In-Ear Monitors

In the Fall of 2017, I acquired a Formlabs Form 1+ SLA printer to start offering CIEMs through Best in the Verse Audio. I built a 3D scanner utilizing photogrammetry technology to scan ear impressions. I then design the shells in 3d modeling software and print the shells on the Form 1+. I use Knowles GV quad balanced armature drivers. The shells are finished with a hypoallergenic lacquer.

Autonomous Remote Control Car

As a part of my NC State class ECE 306 (Intro to Embedded Systems), I built a remote control line-following car. The car uses a Ti MSP430 micro-controller at its heart, with a secondary control board built to control the power, motors, LCD screen, and IoT functionality. The code for the car was written in C. I also wrote a simple Android application to send remote control commands to the car.
